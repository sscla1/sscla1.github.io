what is !buyalert?

!buyalert is a notification-based application for option strikes. the algorithm is constantly updated to keep up with the markets and new data.

PLEASE NOTE:

!buyalert returns strikes with: IV >20%, Delta close to -0.5, Gamma between 50 and 60, and the lowest absolute Theta value. In theory you'd receive a notification, set a buy order at the bid, then once filled set a sell order at the ask. HOWEVER it does not (yet) return strikes that magically have a 100% success rate, or even 50%. Personally, I will use this to supplement other data to ensure a play is aligned with the fundamentals, until !buyalert includes all data as well, & is the one place to meet all stock market needs.

version <2.0 is a BETA. DO NOT use this as the sole reason for any investing decision, this application is at best for finding plays.
